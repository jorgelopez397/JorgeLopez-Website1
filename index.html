Jorge Lopez

Digital Design


I am from South LA, a beautiful city that is surrounded many cultures. Growing up here was hard because my parents always had a financial struggle with bills. It was hard to see a life outside of my neighborhood or even a bright future. Thankfully growing up, my mom taught me the importance of my education and just how far it could take me in life. Once I got to a certain age I started figuring out what I really wanted to do, and after seeing the spark of marketing and brilliant T-shirt designs I decided I wanted to understand the ins and outs of digital design.
In order to accomplish my goal I decided to go to a university. Unfortunately they were not able to offer me the major that I wanted and will futher look for a transer. I am also looking to work with various companies to get my feet wet in how this industry works. After that I want to start branching out with my own designs to see what my value as a designer is. I also have a dream to help local small businesses grow by sharing my designs with them.
